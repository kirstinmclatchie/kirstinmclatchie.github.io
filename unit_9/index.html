About

Creative Design + Sustainable Strategy

Whether you are starting a new project or looking to refresh your brand, your website should be as unique as your business and speak to your audience. That's where I come in.

With experience in graphic desgin and content strategy, I'll help you develop your brand identity, the strategy to connect with your target audience and build you a website to make your brand shine.

I studied graphic design in college, and built my first website nearly 10 years ago. Freelancing allows me to bring my passions together while helping awesome ladypreneures, yogi's, and outdoorsey adventurers build their brands.

After graduating from The Art Institute of California—Orange County, I worked as a designer and art director soaking in inspiration daily at Roadtrip Nation. Currently, I work at Occidental College as Senior Marketing Manager for Web, Digital & Mobile.
